Nintendle

About

This is a Wordle inspired game where the words will be the names of Nintendo characters. Answers are varied in length and you can get more guesses if the answer is longer.

This follows the same rules as Worlde, where green means that the gussed letter is in the correct position, yellow means the letter is in the answer, but in wrong position, and grey means the letter is either not in the word or the letter is used up.

This game allows you to play as many games as you want with the "Play Again" button at the bottom. There is also a link that you can copy at bottom of page that will have a game with your current game's answer as it's answer.
